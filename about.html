My Work

I am currently going into my 4th year of Digital Enterprise Management and Computer Science Minor at the University of Toronto Mississauga. Hence, most of my work revolves around learning how to be an effective manager and foster a productive work environment. Most of my academic work at DEM involves collaborative research, planning, and presentation, which helps me better understand their real work experience and learn how to act in a team and sense group dynamics.

I make use of my academic knowledge and skills in my position at ICCIT Council, where I act as a VP External On-Campus and represent the interests of all ICCIT programs. My responsibilities there include establishing strong and long-term relationships with clubs and organizations on campus by hosting collaborative events and other initiatives. Additionally, I'm responsible for designing a sponsorship package for campus clubs, as well as external organizations, to help us fund the ICCIT Gala - our largest event.

As a Transition Coaching Team Lead at UTM International Student Centre, I also find my academic knowledge and practical skills useful when it comes to project planning, hosting events, or engaging in group work with other staff. IEC puts enormous effort into improving the university experience of international students and I am beyond grateful to be a part of the team!

Hobbies & Interests

I am passionate about game design and interactive media. In my free time, I like to make my own short games in Python or C. I also like playing around with Adobe Illustrator, Blender, and other creative software designing characters and animations. In January 2022, I was lucky to work with a wonderful team on a video game called Pixel Pirate, which won the first place in its category at the annual UTM PyJac hackathon event. In the future, I hope to learn more about Unity and other professional game design tools.

I also enjoy playing and writing my own music. I have been playing guitar since 16 and I love to jam with other musicians and music enthusiasts. In my free time, I like to record song covers or learn new techniques to improve my playing and songwriting. I am currently making songs for my band and learning digital tools for professional mixing and mastering.
