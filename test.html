MAXIM NYANIN

Hello there! My name is Max and I'm a University of Toronto graduate with a degree in Digital Enterprise Management and Computer Science. My academic work revolved around analyzing modern trends in information technology and applying them to maximize efficiency and collaboration of teams and departments in a real-life business environment. Apart from aquiring valuable soft skills like communication, teamwork, and time management, I developed technical efficiency in data analysis, UI/UX and software design, and application testing and threat prevention.

Research Opportunity Program:
Communication Technology & Social Life

Description: a research project that looks into communication technology trends and patterns based on large sets of call and text data with over 40,000 entries. The analysis was performed as a part of an ongoing collaborative study by the University of Toronto.

Tools: R, STATA, NVivo

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • designed an algorithm for analyzing quantitative data
  • performed data cleaning and QA testing
  • created graphs and plots in R studio
  • contributed to team discussions and activities

Family Treasure

Family Treasure is a visual novel / choose-your-own-adventure game made in Unity as a part of a group passion project. The game blends a compelling narrative, immersive visual elements, and interactive puzzles.

Tools: Unity, Unity Fungus, C#, Adobe Photoshop

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • designed game puzzles and challanges using custom C# scripts
  • designed a Unity Fungus flowchart of game states and events
  • helped create visual assets
  • co-wrote the game's narrative

Pixel Pirate

Description: a 2D player-vs-computer video game created by Maxim Nyanin, Adam Kanoun, and Shrivardhan Mishra for UTM PyJaC hackathon 2022. The player must defend their ship against a barrage of cannonballs by using keys to maneuver the pirate character and block the incoming projectiles. In-game physics, rules, and graphics were implemented with OOP in Python.

Tools: Python, pygame, Adobe Photoshop

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • came up with the game idea and challanges
  • implemented the physics of projectile motion and character movement
  • performed QA testing using Python's unittest library
  • designed and kept track of the sprint backlog
